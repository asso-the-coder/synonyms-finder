# synonyms-finder
Implementing Word2Vec model and cosine similarity from scratch to determine if words are similar in context, after training them on large pieces of literature. 

Ultimately achieved >70% accuracy after training on War & Peace and Swann's Way novels.

This algorithm was re-factored towards the 2022 Daisy Hackathon in Toronto, in which I placed as a finalist. It was trained on Amazon product reviews instead but used the same word association techniques to rank products' quality based on the frequency of positive vs. negative sentiment words.
